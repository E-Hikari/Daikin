IoT Module
The Collectivity IoT module was initially developed to enhance the user's experience with their HVAC device, providing more precise control and a more intuitive interaction. However, the potential of the module goes beyond that. It's designed to allow future integrations with a variety of devices, making it a versatile solution for all your home automation needs
AirLink App
The AirLink App is your gateway to smart living. With its user-friendly interface, you can control and monitor your devices from anywhere. Whether you're at home, in the office, or on the go, the AirLink App ensures you're always in control
Chatbot
Our AI-powered chatbot is here to assist you 24/7. Whether you have questions about our products, need troubleshooting tips, or just want to learn more about smart living, our chatbot is ready to help. Experience instant support like never before

With our Personalization AI, your home learns about you. Over time, it adapts to your preferences, routines, and habits, ensuring that your living environment is always in sync with your needs. Experience a truly personalized smart living experience
