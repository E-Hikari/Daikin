Frequently Asked Questions

Navigating through sophisticated HVAC systems can be intricate. This is why we've assembled a comprehensive list of common queries regarding Collectivity, AirLink, and Link. Whether you're keen to understand our system's mechanics, have concerns about data security, or are eager to delve into our features, we're here to clarify. Explore further to address all your inquiries.

  • How does the AirLink app work?

    AirLink is a mobile application that serves as the primary interface for users to control and monitor their HVAC systems. It's designed to be as intuitive as popular messaging apps, allowing users to communicate directly with their system and make adjustments.

  • Who is Link, the chatbot?

    Link is an integrated chatbot within the AirLink app. Instead of navigating through complex menus, users can simply "chat" with Link to control their HVAC system, making the process as simple as sending a text message.

  • How does the AI in Collectivity work?

    Collectivity uses advanced machine learning algorithms to learn and adapt to user preferences. Over time, the system anticipates user needs, making automatic adjustments for optimal comfort and efficiency.
